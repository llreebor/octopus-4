What kind of handyman services do you offer?
Our team can help you by installing artwork and pictures, installing shelves and TV units, setting up curtains, assembling flat-pack furniture, painting and remodelling the living space at your home.
Can I get an estimate or price quote for my job?
Indeed, if you have a specific list of handyman jobs you need, we can estimate the time required and provide a quote using our hourly rate.
What is the difference between a handyman and a contractor?
A reliable handyman is someone who can help with the majority of jobs around the house, whereas a contractor is someone who does more extensive renovation and construction jobs.
Why should I use a reliable handyman?
A reliable handyman guarantees professional service, is skilled in executing the jobs, provides the services in a friendly manner while maintaining everything clean. With a reliable handyman, service will be completed fast and without hassle.
What are some things to prepare to make a handyman service efficient?
Ideally, you would be able to share a complete list of requirements beforehand, so the team can be more efficient when they get into the property.
Do you charge by the job or by the hour?
We charge our customers by the hour. We have a minimum charge of 1 hour, and then after the first hour, we charge in 30 minutes increments.
